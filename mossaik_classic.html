Mossaik Classic
Challenging the Pro photo editors, Mossaik Classic packs a suite of professional tools and offers a perfect platform for those aspiring to make their images standout. Harnessing the power of the GPU, it provides optimal utilization of the hardware and provides real time view of the edits.
Enjoy powerful one touch edit, Mossaik Classic has a an inventory of carefully crafted presets to save you time and give your editing a head start. You may fine tune the image further as the edits are Non Destructive.


Emphasize the image components to make your point… use the local tools that apply pixel level detailed edits only in the area of selection. And add as many layers you like so make multiple localized edits, layer by layer.


And the local tools offered match the best in the industry… use the Gradient Mask to select a portion and feather its strength to make a smooth transition. Don’t forget the instant image access via the filmstrip, showcasing real time edits.
Enjoy the pixel level control with cutting edge technology running behind the scene. A simple and functional user interface hides a beast that delivers all the necessary controls, seamlessly.
